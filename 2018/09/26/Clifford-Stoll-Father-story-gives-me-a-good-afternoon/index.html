Clifford Stoll Father story gives me a good afternoon

I am going through a tough time just like everybody in this channel. When I had attack by depression, I feel tired and numb, nothing usual can comfort me. Then I found it quite amazingly useful to watch a good story, such as Clifford Stoll Father story.

Clifford is a scientist and he gives really interesting lectures on Youtube. His father was a bar attender. When Cliff was six, he asked his father if he can have some beer, the father said no and replied he could try again when he gets older. When Cliff turned to twelve, he asked the same question, the father still said no. Finally Cliff turned 18, he asked his father if he can have some alcohol and he father still said no. Cliff have never asked the question again and he had never been close with alcohol. He said he always remember the good feelings when he stayed with his father, so he lacked of interests in alcohol. See the video here https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BDEo5XpZcXo

I felt so warmed after reading the story and the energy seems to come back. Hopes it can help you too.
