letter to Jo 01

Newly Married Wife in Depress, Try to Cheer Up by Writing Little Stories

Hi Jo,it has been a while since we talked last time. Let me introduce myself again.

About Me

I was thrilled writing to you. There are 5 letters that I wrote to you about last year. Their topic is as below:

  • 2018 March, I dislike the first job as a drug sales woman.
  • 2018 May, I resigned the sales job after 3 months of effort.
  • 2018 June, I got married with my boyfriend who just became a pediatric doctor.
  • 2018 Sep, I moved to the nearby city of my husband because of the money issue.
  • 2018 Aug, I got a wonderful job and did a good work as a designer.
  • 2018 Nov, unfortunately the company expects me to go to BEIJING. I decide to stay with my husband and lost job again.
  • Right now, I had used to live alone for almost half a year.

In the past several months, I was preparing for a totally brand new job which drains a lot of energy from me. Although I was financially secured and always have company of my husband.But I still felt tired and breakdown.

I have tried several things to soothe my nerve:

  • doing yoga
  • read novel
  • cook at home
  • walk in the park
  • sleep

As you can see, I am doing ok with all sorts of self-pump activity. But this week, I sleep for more than 10 hours a day. And I just lost my appetite. All negative thoughts come to attack me. I felt so bad.

That’s why it comes to me, that maybe I should write to you and bring you some little story which I read. It’s like a secret game.

Small Story : Diapause

So today I am about introduce introduce a word to you, which is “diapause”.

I read word from the book Caves and Cave Life. It was written by Philip Chapman and published in 1993.

It’s an interesting book which introduces a whole different world from our human society. Life is mysterious and tough for underground animals. For example, they prefer to live in moist environment instead of pleasantly cool environment. It’s a brutal choice actually. Because they can only get enough food from the underground flood. The underground flood cannot be managed as we do upon the ground. And what’s the worse, the flood came in the spring and summer. They can only survive after the flood. So they choose to sleep, not participating in this brutal disaster until late summer. But in our up ground world, we breed and prosper just during the spring and the summer. For the poor guys underground, they don’t even have to spring. They just have to pretend to die in spring and wake up on a cold day in early autumn. They have to reproduce very quickly, and they have to sleep again since winter is coming.

I was an animal in diapause too.Hope you like my small story.

Jennifer.
